注册麻烦的顶级域名后缀
=====================
### .de 
For registration of a .de domain, your Administrative Contact must be a resident of Germany.

### .es
You must provide a valid identification number to register a .es domain. Please note that this domain does not support free changes to contact information after registration.

### .fr
.FR domains - Please choose european country for 'FR' tld domain registration in Registrant Contact.
